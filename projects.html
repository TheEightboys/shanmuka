Lean Process Optimization of IFAK Assembly Using DMAIC

Applied the DMAIC framework alongside Process Mapping, PFMEA, and Spaghetti Chart analysis to identify workflow inefficiencies in the IFAK (Individual First Aid Kit) assembly line. Materials Engineering Product Design Process Selection

Systematic Material Selection & Process Optimization for Snowboard Core Design

Applied Ashby's materials selection framework to identify an optimal snowboard core material under mechanical, environmental, and economic constraints. Screened and ranked CFRP, GFRP, Aluminum 5056, and Magnesium AE42-F using material indices and performance charts. Implemented a weighted decision matrix (70% performance, 30% cost). Although Mg AE42-F showed superior mechanical performance, conventional wood cores were validated as more practical for large-scale production.

06
Structural Optimization FEA Computational Optimization

Topology Optimization of Fork End in Knuckle Joint Using Multiple Algorithms

Reduced weight of a fork end in a knuckle joint while maintaining structural integrity under 50 N tensile load. Compared three topology optimization algorithms in ANSYS Workbench — SCP, Optimality Criteria, and MOGA (NSGA-II). Optimality Criteria achieved the best balance: 0.62992 kg in 24 minutes (14 iterations). SCP yielded similar mass at higher computational cost; Genetic Algorithm was the least efficient at 1.0082 kg in 43 minutes.
