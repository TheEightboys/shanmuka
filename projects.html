01. Lean Process Optimization of IFAK Assembly Using DMAIC

Applied the DMAIC framework alongside Process Mapping, PFMEA, and Spaghetti Chart analysis to identify workflow inefficiencies in the IFAK (Individual First Aid Kit) assembly line. Key improvements included optimizing inventory rotation—reducing RPN from 360 to 90—and redesigning part retrieval flow, resulting in a 29% reduction in operator motion. A refined SOP was developed to standardize best practices.

02. Digital Twin–Based Manufacturing Plant Layout Design & Optimization

Designed and optimized a manufacturing plant layout for bell and bearing housing production within an $8 million capital budget. Using Siemens Tecnomatix Plant Simulation, developed a digital twin to evaluate machine placement and production bottlenecks. Achieved 67% machine utilization, reduced wait time to 22%, and limited block time to 11% through iterative simulation and strategic machine repositioning.

03. Body-in-White (BIW) Structural Design & Finite Element Analysis

Designed and structurally validated a Body-in-White (BIW) system for a new vehicle platform using SolidWorks. Performed comprehensive finite element analysis in HyperMesh evaluating bending stiffness, torsional rigidity, front and rear crashworthiness, and limit load conditions. The analysis ensured compliance with safety standards while optimizing structural integrity and load distribution.

04. Silicone Mold Metal Casting Design & Gating System Engineering (ZAMAK-3 Batrang)

End-to-end design and analysis of a silicone mold casting process to manufacture a ZAMAK-3 Batrang replica. Designed a non-pressurized 1:2:1.5 gating system to minimize turbulence and flow defects. Performed detailed calculations for pouring rate, sprue/runner dimensions, ingate sizing, riser design, and solidification time using Chvorinov's rule. Mold and casting assembly modeled in SolidWorks.

05. Systematic Material Selection & Process Optimization for Snowboard Core Design

Applied Ashby's materials selection framework to identify an optimal snowboard core material under mechanical, environmental, and economic constraints. Screened and ranked CFRP, GFRP, Aluminum 5056, and Magnesium AE42-F using material indices and performance charts. Implemented a weighted decision matrix (70% performance, 30% cost). Although Mg AE42-F showed superior mechanical performance, conventional wood cores were validated as more practical for large-scale production.

06. Topology Optimization of Fork End in Knuckle Joint Using Multiple Algorithms

Reduced weight of a fork end in a knuckle joint while maintaining structural integrity under 50 N tensile load. Compared three topology optimization algorithms in ANSYS Workbench — SCP, Optimality Criteria, and MOGA (NSGA-II). Optimality Criteria achieved the best balance: 0.62992 kg in 24 minutes (14 iterations). SCP yielded similar mass at higher computational cost; Genetic Algorithm was the least efficient at 1.0082 kg in 43 minutes.

07. Influence of Function Representation on Product Design Creativity and Reliability

Literature review examining how function representation methods influence the creative and analytical phases of product design. Analyzed function trees, function structures, morphological analysis, FMEA, value engineering, and hierarchical FAD methodologies. Key insight: structured function representation reduces design fixation and improves reliability when integrated with risk-based tools. Proposed need for resource-efficient functional modeling frameworks for SMEs.

08. Agri-Bot: RF-Controlled Dual-Wheel Agricultural Assistance Robot

Designed a compact dual-wheel robotic platform for agricultural tasks such as plowing and watering, controlled via RF communication. Integrated an Arduino UNO microcontroller, stepper motors for motion control, actuator mechanisms, and a lead-acid battery for onboard power. Combines embedded systems, mechanical actuation, and wireless control into a cost-effective automation solution for small-scale farming.

09. Design and Simulation of Convergent LPG Burner with Diffuser

Designed and simulated a convergent LPG burner with modified geometry to improve combustion efficiency. Modeled the burner in SolidWorks and analyzed using ANSYS Fluent/CFX simulations. Integrated a diffuser to induce a swirling effect enhancing fuel–air mixing. Simulations conducted across various hole angles and configurations to maximize thermal efficiency. Final step involves brass fabrication and water boiling test for empirical validation.
